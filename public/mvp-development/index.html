Our students want to build your APP/MVP.

Our students have just gone through an intensive 7-week online coding bootcamp and some of them are trying to add a few independent projects to their portfolio as they prepare for job interviews.

If you have an idea for a web app that you don't know how to build, why not become their first client?

Most students will do it for free, but you can offer to cover their tuition fees ($497) and give them more of an incentive to choose your project over others.

If you're a hiring partner, you can also use a sponsored project to access the suitability of our students for your job.

Please fill out this form, leave your contact details and we'll get back to you if your project is chosen by any of our students.

If you decide to sponsor a student's tuition fees, a payment is only required if the student successfully completes your project.
